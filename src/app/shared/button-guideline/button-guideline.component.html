Profile Guidelines Ambassador Guidelines

We want to ensure you receive the best experience being a registered member of PromptHealth. To do so, we are enforcing profile guidelines to ensure our network remains professional and suitable for our wide range of clients.

Before setting up your profile, please take a moment to review these guidelines:

  • Ensure your real first and last names are entered correctly, with no nicknames or symbols.
  • State your professional organization and any certification in order for us to verify you.
  • Please ensure you are fully clothed in any images you upload.
  • Please do not use any profanity or offensive language in your description. If found, PromptHealth reserves the right to suspend your profile.
  • Do not advertise any third-party products. Your profile should only be used to showcase the services you provide as a professional.

If you have any questions about creating your profile, please reach out to us at info@prompthealth.ca or at (778) 316-3124.

As a PromptHealth brand ambassador, you will:

  • Post using resources in our Social Media Kit and tag us (@prompthealth)
  • Be featured on our social media! Create educational video content that you are passionate about (such as an Instagram reel), and tag us when you post it! We will then repost your content featuring you as a registered provider which will bring new followers to your account.
  • Share your personal referral link, found on your PromptHealth profile, through different channels (e.g. Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, WhatsApp, via email, etc.). Each time someone uses your link to sign up, you will receive a credit toward our premium membership plans which you can redeem later on.
